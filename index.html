Goals

I have 3 goals as a first-year Computer Science student at Staffordshire University.

  1. Build a solid understanding of Computer Science concepts

    I reckon building a solid understanding of Computer Science concepts in my first-year is essential. In order to achieve this, I will ask questions actively where I don't understand, I will do research about it and practise it in my own time. I believe that this will help me in my second year significantly. I think lacking fundamentals will be a big obstacle in order to be successful.

  2. Get used to group works

    Getting used to working in groups is crucial for my career. It is not just about being good at programming. I do not have that much experience in group working projects and I am lacking communication skills, which are the most important things in group work. I want to acquire this skill through my group work projects at University which directly contributes to my career job.

  3. Develop my interest in programming outside of University

    To develop my programming skills and get proficient with it, it is not enough just following lectures and tutorial tasks. I reckon to work on my programming skills outside of University and working on personal projects are the most efficient way of developing my programming skills. Therefore, I want to create a habit of practising programming skills regularly and I want to try working on personal projects, which interests me.
